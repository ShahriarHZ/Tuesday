# 🌿 KeenKeeper — Personal Relationship Manager
> **"Don't just network. Nurture."**

**KeenKeeper** is a lightweight, privacy-focused personal CRM designed to help you maintain meaningful connections. In a world of digital noise, KeenKeeper acts as your "personal shelf," tracking the people who matter most and providing data-driven insights into your social health.

---

### ✨ Key Features
* **Interaction Timeline:** A centralized log for Calls, Texts, and Video chats with intelligent filtering.
* **Friendship Analytics:** Visualized data ratios (via Recharts) that show how you’re communicating with your inner circle.
* **Smart Scheduling:** Track contact frequency and get visual cues when it's time to reach back out.
* **Privacy First:** Built on local persistence logic, ensuring your personal relationship data stays on your device.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** Next.js 16 (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS
* **UI Components:** DaisyUI & Lucide Icons
* **Visualization:** Recharts (Doughnut Analytics)
* **State & Storage:** React Hooks & LocalStorage API
* **Performance:** Optimized with Turbopack for lightning-fast development

---

### 💡 The Technical Challenge
As a **CSE student**, I focused on solving the "Hydration Mismatch" common in Next.js when working with client-side storage. By implementing a custom mounting strategy, I ensured that the **Friendship Analytics** doughnut chart renders smoothly without layout shift, providing a production-grade user experience.
